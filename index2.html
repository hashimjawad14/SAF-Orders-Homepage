How Does It Work?

Through our services, take your employees on a seamless order & tracking journey!

QR Code Order & Payment

Scan, browse, and order instantly from any desk

▼

Employees can scan a QR code at their desk to browse the complete menu, choose their favorite beverages and snacks, and place orders instantly without needing to call or wait for staff.

Each desk has a unique QR code that automatically identifies the location, room number, and desk ID, ensuring accurate delivery every time.

Real-Time Dashboard

Orders appear instantly on kitchen displays

▼

Orders instantly appear on the assigned tea boy's dashboard with complete details including desk location, room number, items ordered, and any special requests in the notes section.

Order Fulfillment & Updates

Track every order from preparation to delivery

▼

Tea boys can easily update order status from 'Received' to 'In Progress' to 'Completed' with just a tap. This keeps employees informed about their order's progress.

Statistics & Administration

Comprehensive insights and performance metrics

▼

One dashboard to manage operations and track performance. Usage, delivery times, ratings, and team performance — all in one place.
