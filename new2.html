How Does It Work?

A seamless service journey from request to delivery.

QR Code Service Requests

Scan, browse, and request instantly from any desk

▼

Employees scan a QR code at their desk to browse available services and place requests instantly — no calls, no apps, no delays.

Each desk has a unique QR code that automatically identifies the location, ensuring accurate and fast delivery every time.

Real-Time Dashboard

Requests appear instantly on staff dashboards

▼

Requests appear instantly on the service staff dashboard with full details — desk location, room number, items requested, and notes.

Order Fulfillment & Updates

Track every request from preparation to delivery

▼

Staff update request status from “Received” to “In Progress” to “Completed,” keeping employees informed in real time.

Statistics & Administration

Comprehensive insights and performance metrics

▼

One dashboard to manage operations and track performance — usage, response times, and service quality, all in one place.
